About Licious Fish & Seafood

Fish & seafood are a staple food and part of daily meals in several parts of the world because of its high nutritional quotient.

From appetizers to main courses, fish & seafood can be the perfect choice to elevate your menu, whether you are planning a regular family lunch, a dinner party with friends or a holiday buffet.

At Licious, we bring you only the freshest fish & seafood that is guaranteed by our supply of daily catch from local fishermen. Our experts carefully pick the best fish, which are then cut and thoroughly cleaned before being delivered to your doorstep. The freshwater and seawater fish can be used in different dishes to prepare an enjoyable meal for yourself and your loved ones.

Our wide range of fish and seafood includes:

  • White Pomfret (Paplet) – Whole – Cleaned – A fish variant that is loved by most people, the flattened seawater fish is offered whole. It is thoroughly cleaned and gutted. White Pomfret has a delicate texture and moderate flavour. The fish can be used to make curries, fish bake, fish grill, or masala fish fry.
  • Prawns Medium w/o Tail (Cleaned and Deveined) – Medium-sized freshwater Prawns from Licious are de-shelled, deveined and thoroughly cleaned with the head and tail removed, making them ready to cook. Rich in proteins, the prawns have a mild sweet taste. This seafood makes for a great starter. It can also be added to curries and biryanis.
  • Sea Bass Curry Cut – The neatly cut Sea Bass Curry Cut is best suited to make fish curries. With a mild sweet taste and firm texture, the Sea Bass pieces can also be used for pan-frying, grilling, or baking. Make delicious fish curry or pan fry masala at home using these curry cut pieces.
  • Freshwater Catla – Bengali Cut – An absolute favourite in the eastern part of India, this lean bony fish fillet from Licious is offered without the head and with the tail. Cut in Bengali style, the strong flavour and moist texture of the Catla is perfect for a slow-simmer fish curry or soup.
  • Basa Fillet – Freshwater fish, the Basa is sourced from the Indian waters. The fillet is sliced lengthwise, parallel to the spine along one side of the fish. It has a medium-firm texture and moderate flavour. Basa Fillet is best enjoyed when grilled, baked, or masala fried. It is an excellent choice for the ones who are new to eating seafood.
  • Red Snapper Cubes (Medium) – The Red Snapper is cut into boneless cubes and offered ready to cook. A highly nutritious fish, it is low in saturated fats and has a mild sweet taste. This makes it quite a favourite in many households. The Red Snapper is a good addition in salads, stir-fries, grills, or even as a good topping for pasta.
  • Squid Rings/ Kanava/ Kadama – Delicately sliced crosswise, Squid Rings at Licious are thoroughly cleaned and ready to cook. Squids have a delicate sweet flavour and a slightly chewy texture. The ready-to-cook rings can be used to make deep-fried Calamari or Spicy Masala Squid Fry.

FAQs
  • Should fish be washed before cooking?
    The fish bought from Licious is thoroughly cleaned before it is packed to be sent to you. You do not need to extensively wash it before cooking. You can, however, run cold water over it once before you start your cooking process. It is important to note that invisible contamination like bacteria cannot be removed by washing the fish. The only way to ensure such bacteria or pathogens are eliminated is by properly cooking the fish. You do not need to worry about all of this when you get your fish from Licious, as it is handled extremely hygienically and properly cleaned already.
  • Can I consume fish daily?
    Yes, fish and other seafood can be consumed daily. It can be a part of your healthy and balanced diet. Unless you are allergic to a fish variant, it is a great food for daily intake.

We will sell only the meat that we would eat ourselves.

At Licious, we’re big meat-lovers. And by big, we mean huge. So when it comes to the meat we put on your plate, we’r extremely picky. Every single product is handpicked by a team with years of experience.

If it’s not fresh, we won’t sell it

For meat to stay fresh and retain its natural juices, it needs to be stored at a temperature between 0° and 5°C. We maintain this temperature from the time we procure the product to cleaning, cutting and storing it, until it leaves for delivery. And even when it’s out for delivery, we keep it chilled right up to your doorstep.Did we mention that we’re obsessed?

We will charge only for what you buy

Doesn’t everyone do this? Not really. Most other places first weigh the meat, then cut up the pieces, and throw out the parts which aren’t fit to eat, such as offal, gizzard, wingtips, etc. But you still pay based on the original weight even though what you finally get is 10% to 30% less
